ALBUMS:
  • Twenty One Pilots

    • This is the first album the band ever made. It's mostly piano based on this album. This is the groups beginning when Tyler first began to put his thoughts into words. My favorite songs on this album are March to the Sea and Isles of Flightless Birds.

  • Regional At Best

    • This is the band's second album. The album's songs vary with style but the central purpose of their music is to explain thoughts that really everybody has. My favorite songs on this album are Forest and Kitchen Sink.

  • Vessle

    • This is the band's third album. The album's songs are a lot more heavy on the piano and vocals. The ocassional shouts in the song sort of wake you up a bit and help you understand the thoughts that are going on in not only Tyler's head but really any person. My all time favorite song Migraine is on this album along with their classic Trees.
