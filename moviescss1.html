Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope

This movie series takes place in a galaxy under the wicked rule of the Empire In this movie the protaganist Luke Skywalker joins forces with the Rebel Alliance, a group determined to bring down the Empire and restore order in the galaxy. This movie is a timeless tale of adventure in a galaxy far far away...
